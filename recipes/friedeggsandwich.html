Fried Egg Sandwich

About

Quick, easy and healthy recipe for breakfasts and lunch. Something I made many times in Tsurugashima.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Slim slices of bread
  • Oil
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic seasoning

Steps

  1. Oil the pan and turn heat to high.
  2. Crack your eggs. (yolk breaking is fine)
  3. Use spatula to keep sides from sticking.
  4. Flip once one side is cooked, and at the same time toast bread.
  5. Crack the yolks while cooking second side and season.
